Tibetan Buddhist Bell

Buddhist monks ring these bells during meditation, concentrating on the sound and how it changes during their practice. These bells can be used to signal that it is time to meditate and can be used during practice to provide a focus for the meditation.

Chinese Knot

These decorative knots often decorate posts, walls, and doors during Spring Festival, also known as the Chinese New Year. The knot represents the infinite knot of prosperity and happiness that according to traditional folklore is prayed for with the coming of each new year. A common blessing/saying during the new year is gongxi facai, which roughly means "congratulations, accumulate wealth!"

Price: $10.00
